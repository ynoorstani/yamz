A Brief Background

My interest in web development stemmed from my passion for gaming. Between developing websites and playing video games, I admittedly spend most of my time in front of a screen. Some of my passions are writing clean code, creating awesome user experiences and filling up a fresh cup of coffee.

I am a beginner programmer from Northern Virginia. For the past few months I have been enrolled in an online bootcamp, called Bloc. Bloc is a renowned online bootcamp course which provides a coding mentor to teach you for up to 3-4 months in Front-End Web Development. I will graduating the program in the end of June!

In my free time I like to code and pair program with my friends. My number one goal is enroll into Hack Reactor and someday become a Software Engineer.

I will keep practicing my craft, and feel blessed to be working with substantially talented people.
